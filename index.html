Xenoblade Chronicles is a semi-open world action RPG developed by MonolithSoft and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Wii and 3DS. The game follows Shulk as he travels across the corpses of the enormous titans Bionis and Mechonis that make up his world as he sets out to get revenge against the Mechon, mechanical beings of Mechonis that are set to destroy all biological life on Bionis.

Explore vast plains, glowing swamps, and humid forests as you ascend the Bionis to seek answers for why the Mechon wish to destroy everything.

As of May 29th, 2020, Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition has released on the Nintendo Switch. Featuring remade graphics, numerous quality of life improvements, and an entire new post-game story, there is no better time to get into Xenoblade Chronicles.
This is a fan website dedicated to helping those new to the game with Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition and even those returning veterans who wish to experience the game in full HD fully complete every aspect of the game. With 480 quests to clear, 63 Heart-to-Hearts to experience, and 157 unique monsters to fight, achieving 100% completion is no easy feat!

Each of the pages listed above leads to a completion checklist to help you keep track of everything you've accomplished. Once something is clear, check it off and the website will keep track of your completion for you!
